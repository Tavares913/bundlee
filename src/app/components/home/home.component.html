Collections Add Individuals to Sets

Add individuals to collections in order to recommend them in sets to your friends. You can search other people's collections and favourite any you come accross too!

Individuals Media such as anime, manga, video games, movies, and TV shows

Search for any anime (ex. one piece), manga (ex. berserk), video games (ex. hollow knight [on the nintendo switch]), or tv shows (ex. breaking bad), see their info and ratings, and add them to collections. Info is obtained using the following APIs: anilist (anime), mangadex (manga), metacritic (video games), IMDB (movies/tv shows)
